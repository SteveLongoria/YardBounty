YardBounty

Fresh, Chemical-free Produce Grown & Harvested In Your Backyard Without You Lifting A Finger

YardBounty is a decentralized application created to help connect landowners with natural farmers who grow food by the square yard and receive monthly steem payments in return. Subscription can be cancelled by either party at any time. The farmers are inspired by the teachings of Masanobu Fukuoka. YardBounty is currently in development, enter your email address below to stay up to date on development.

Find a local farmer to grow food in your backyard without a middleman taking a cut

It's never been easier to find a local natural farmer to grow chemical-free produce in your backyard for a small monthly fee. YardBounty is a decentralized open-source platform to connect you with a reputable farmer while only take a small 1% cut on transactions to fund ongoing developer costs.

All Users Have A Reputation Score

To ensure that all users act in each others best interest, we have a rating system so you know which users are the most reputable. This means

Farmer

This user gets paid in Steem for growing food on other peoples land. Paid out monthly. Learn more here.

Homeowner

These users have land that they wish was being utilized to grow fresh, chemical-free produce and are willing to pay for it.

Arbiter

Since YardBounty is a decentralized, autonomous application, there is no administration to handle disputes between 2 parties. If a dispute does arise, you can enlist the services of a YardBounty Arbiter to solve the dispute in a fair manner.

Affiliate

This user gets a recurring percentage for each user they sign-up to the platform, whether it's a farmer, homeowner or arbiter.
